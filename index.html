Where cybersecurity meets artificial intelligence

The rapid digitalization of society, the proliferation of IoT and cyber‑physical systems, and the growing adoption of artificial intelligence have introduced new security challenges. CDPIDS-2026 focuses on how intelligent systems can detect, prevent, and respond to cyber threats while protecting data and ensuring system integrity.

AI‑Driven Security

Intelligent threat detection, adversarial machine learning, behavioral biometrics, and AI‑based attack prevention for resilient digital ecosystems.

Data Protection & Privacy

Secure storage, processing and transmission of data, personal data protection, cryptography, and privacy‑preserving technologies.

Resilient Systems

Security of IoT, cloud, edge and cyber‑physical systems, fault tolerance, and protection of safety‑critical digital infrastructures.

Workshop objectives

Address emerging threats

Examine current challenges and emerging threats in cybersecurity and the protection of intelligent digital environments.

AI for defense

Explore AI‑based methods for attack detection and prevention, and study technologies for secure data storage, processing and transmission.

Secure modern infrastructures

Discuss security issues in cloud computing, IoT and cyber‑physical systems, alongside legal, ethical, and organizational aspects.

Share practical findings

Present practical case studies and research findings in digital security to advance the state of the art.

Foster collaboration

Strengthen cooperation among universities, research centers, and industry across the cybersecurity community.

Human‑centric security

Examine the human dimension of cybersecurity, including social engineering defense and organizational resilience.

Call for Papers — topics of interest

We invite submissions on, but not limited to, the following topics.

AI in Cybersecurity

Artificial intelligence in cybersecurity, machine learning security, and adversarial AI.

Detection & Response

Intrusion detection and prevention systems, malware analysis, and ransomware protection.

Authentication & Privacy

Behavioral biometrics, continuous authentication, personal data protection and privacy.

Network, Cloud & Edge

Network, cloud and edge security; security of IoT and cyber‑physical systems.

Cryptography

Cryptography, secure communications, threat intelligence and cyber risk management.

Forensics & DevSecOps

Digital forensics and cybercrime investigation, secure software development and DevSecOps.

Ethics & Law

Ethical and legal aspects of cybersecurity in intelligent digital systems.

Human Factor

Human‑centric cybersecurity and social engineering defense.
